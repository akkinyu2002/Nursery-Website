About the nursery

A local plant business built around healthy stock, honest guidance, and practical service.

Lumbini Nursery and Plant service supports customers in Murgiya, Sainamaina, Butwal, and wider Rupandehi with plants that make homes and business spaces feel more alive.

Our story

Rooted in local trust

The nursery serves everyday local needs: healthy plants for home gardens, easy indoor greens for families and offices, fruit plants for practical growing, and accessories that help customers finish the look in one visit.

Instead of feeling like a generic catalog, the business focuses on practical buying decisions, helpful guidance, and plants suited to real Nepal-based homes and business spaces.

Mission

Make plant buying simple, trustworthy, and inspiring.

The goal is to help customers beautify their homes, balconies, courtyards, and workplaces with quality plants and support that feels clear and reliable.

Landscaping, garden setup, greenery styling, and accessory support help the nursery serve customers who want more than a single potted plant.
